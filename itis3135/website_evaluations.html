Website Evaluations

- Pacific Northwest X-ray Inc.-

They sell radiology equipment, supplies, parts and accessories

  • C: The contrast is really bad. The background makes it hard to read the text.

  • R: They achieve repitition with their list of links to their products using the same icon. It doesn't look good but it is repitition.

  • A: The alignment for the most part seems fine. There are some areas where images are technically aligned but it doesn't work for those specific elements.

  • P: Overall, I believe that the proximity is alright. Elements that relate to a certain product are close together.

- Ninja Kiwi -

Video game developer and publisher

  • C: I think that contrast is managed well on this page. There is some text over the images but it has a semi-transparent background that makes it readable
  • R: The font stays the same for most of the website which makes it all feel well put together. The colors stay consistent throughout the website too.
  • A: This is done well because all the elements under the banner are well aligned even though every element isn't the same.
  • P: Each section is well placed. The games and news section are grouped together without needing an explicit outline.
